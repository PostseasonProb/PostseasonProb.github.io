KBO League Postseason Odds


You can check the KBO Postseason odds every day simulated by Pythagorean winning percentage.

Sources for all stats are KBO Website.

All interactive plots are generated by using Plotly.

You can check KBO postseason odds graphs on the season summary pages.    Check out 2023

If you want to know about the simulation procedure, check out THIS BLOG(KOREAN).

All game results are applied at 12:15 a.m.(KST) every day.


If you want to know the odds of winning the Korean series for each PS game from the 2016 season, check out the following page. 경기별 PS 우승 확률(KOREAN)





pyth WL and Adj.pyth mean the Pythagorean winning percentage and the Pythagorean winning percentage considering the opponent's strength, respectively.
