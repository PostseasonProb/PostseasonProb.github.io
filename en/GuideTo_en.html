User Guide



1. You can check every day's postseason advancement probability by moving the cursor.



2. You can display a specific team by double-clicking that team in the legend on the right. Double-click anywhere on the graph if you want to go back to its normal.



3. You can clear or display a specific team from the graph by clicking on that team in the legend.



4. You can zoom in by dragging the graph you want to enlarge. Click 'Reset Zoom' button if you want to go back to its original form.



5. You can select the date range and check the postseason odds graph. If you want to go back to its original then click the 'Reset Date Range' button. The 'Reset Zoom' button can not make the date range to its original.
